Mission

M

At School 71, our mission is to provide a nurturing and challenging educational environment where students can grow into confident, creative, and responsible individuals. We aim to foster a love for lifelong learning by offering a well-rounded curriculum that balances academic excellence, critical thinking, and personal growth. Our goal is to equip students with the skills they need to thrive in an ever-changing world and become empathetic, engaged citizens of the global community.

Vision

V

Our vision is to be a leading educational institution that inspires students to reach their full potential, both academically and personally. We strive to create a school culture that embraces innovation, inclusivity, and leadership development. By preparing our students with the skills, values, and knowledge to face future challenges, we hope to shape future generations who will positively impact their communities and the world at large.

History

H

School 71 has a rich history of academic excellence and community engagement. Established in 1997, our school has been a pillar of education in Uzbekistan for decades. Over the years, we have grown and adapted to meet the changing needs of our students while maintaining our strong commitment to academic rigor and holistic development. With deep roots in tradition and a forward-thinking approach, School 71 continues to evolve, preparing students for success in a globalized world.
