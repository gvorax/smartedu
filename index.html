Welcome to School 71 - A Place for Bright Futures

Our History: Milestones of Excellence

Since its founding in 1997, School 71 has consistently demonstrated a commitment to educational excellence. Over the years, we have achieved several important milestones, reflecting our growth and impact in the community and beyond.

2018

By 2018, numerous alumni of School 71 had achieved great success in various fields, from academia to business and the arts, further proving the enduring impact of our educational philosophy.

2015

We invested in major infrastructure upgrades, including state-of-the-art classrooms, labs, and sports facilities, creating an environment conducive to learning and development.

2014

School 71 was honored with an award for Innovation in Education, recognizing our commitment to integrating modern teaching methods and technology into our curriculum.

2012

To foster well-rounded individuals, we expanded our extracurricular offerings, including arts, sports, and leadership programs, ensuring students had opportunities to excel beyond the classroom.

2010

Our students achieved top results in national examinations, placing School 71 among the highest-performing schools in the country. This marked a new level of academic distinction.

2007

School 71 launched an international exchange program, partnering with schools abroad to broaden students’ perspectives and enhance cultural understanding.

2004

Recognizing the importance of STEM, we introduced advanced science and technology programs, providing students with cutting-edge resources and opportunities for innovation.

2002

In 2002, School 71 received its first national award for outstanding academic performance, distinguishing itself as a leader in education throughout Uzbekistan.
